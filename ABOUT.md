`memory-bench` runs five agent-memory architectures on a single TiDB Cloud Serverless cluster. One `memories` table. One hybrid-retrieval SQL statement that fuses vector, full-text, JSON, and structured predicates in a single `ORDER BY`. Swapping architectures is a `WHERE approach = ?` change. Less surface area than any alternative stack — fewer systems to synchronize, fewer places for the comparison to go wrong. Evaluated against LongMemEval oracle slices with Gemini 3.1 Flash-Lite.
